A daily life in AWALIFE

This Isn’t Just Any Average Job

We are a team united by love for animals and we aim to achieve a higher goal in animal medical field and embrace a harmonious future between animals and human beings. We fully respect and believe employees’ personal thoughts and abilities, everyone here has a chance to play a crucial role.
What we offer

Open roles

From Awalife to world, let’s create value for animal health together!

Sales Manager

Overall purpose: This position is responsible for managing related distributors, selling and promoting Awalife’s products in designated region, to achieve the financial goal.

Key responsibilities:

  • Selling andpromoting Morphological Analyzer product range in local
  • Responsible for channel construction, development, maintenance and management.
  • Organize and launch regional marketing activities

Requirements:

  • Over one year sales experience of animal medical
  • Excellent communication skills, organization and coordination ability
  • Strong teamwork spirit
  • Bachelor degree or above, having related work experience or majored in veterinary is preferred
  • Good adaptability of business trip

Senior Software Engineer

Overall purpose: This position is responsible for the development of product’s system and AI platform tools, to assist test engineers to analyze and solve problems in actual development.

Key responsibilities:

  • Responsible for the development and maintenance of POCT system software, develop and transplant on Windows and Linux based on QT.
  • Responsible for the development of AI platform tools, and assist the AI team in rapid annotation and training.
  • Responsible for embedded ARM driver and BSP development.
  • Completed software module design, document writing, code writing and system debugging according to system design requirements

Requirements:

  • Computer-related major, over 3 years of relevant experience, bachelor degree or above.
  • Proficient in C/C++, have a good sense of object-oriented programming and have good programming habits.
  • Proficient in QT application development framework and plug-in mechanism, familiar with QT drawing/use of various communication libraries, with QT cross-platform development experience (Win/Linux)
  • Have relevant knowledge of embedded underlying software development
  • Goal-oriented, with strong learning ability, systematic analysis ability, good teamwork and communication skills.

AI Engineer

Overall purpose:We are looking for an experienced AI engineer to be responsible for developing our pet diagnostics AI platform. The platform will utilize advanced AI technology to provide accurate and fast pet diagnosis services for veterinarians.

Key responsibilities:

  • Responsible for the development and maintenance of the pet diagnostics AI platform, including model selection, training, optimization, and deployment.
  • Work closely with the product team to understand the business requirements, and conduct demand analysis and functional design of the AI platform.
  • Optimize the performance of the AI platform to improve the diagnostic accuracy and efficiency.
  • Track and solve the technical problems arising during the development and operation of the AI platform.
  • Write and maintain technical documentation, including algorithm design, model training, and test reports.

Key responsibilities:

  • At least 3 years of AI development experience, familiar with machine learning and deep learning and other related technologies.
  • Familiar with programming languages and frameworks like Python, TensorFlow and PyTorch.
  • Familiar with common data processing tools, such as NumPy, Pandas, etc.
  • Excellent problem analysis and problem solving ability, able to think independently and solve complex technical problems.
  • Good communication and teamwork ability to effectively cooperate with the product team, technical team and other relevant team members.
  • Have a strong interest in the pet medical industry and are willing to learn and explore in this field.
