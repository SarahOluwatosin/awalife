AI-100Vet Morphological Analyzer

Al-100Vet Morphological Analyzer is an intelligent diagnostic device tailored for veterinary applications. It supports automatic analysis of four sample types—blood, feces, urine sediment, and pleural fluid—meeting the diverse clinical needs of more than 10 species.

Broad Scope of Application

The Al-100Vet Morphological Analyzer is designed to meet diagnostic and testing needs across a wide range of animal species—from common pets such as cats and dogs to exotic animals like birds and reptiles. It currently supports species including dogs, cats, rabbits, turtles, birds, parrots, snakes, and lizards.  

Compatible with four sample types

The Al-100Vet Morphological Analyzer supports four core sample types: blood, feces, urine sediment, and pleural fluid.
  • Blood:Requiring only 10μL of blood, the AI-100Vet captures 200,000 to 500,000 cells in real-time imaging and completes a full blood smear analysis within 8 minutes. By conducting high-precision morphological analysis of key indicators such as red blood cells, white blood cells, and platelets, the device provides robust clinical data support.
  • Feces:The system automates the entire process, including sample preparation, image recognition, and data analysis—significantly reducing human error and oversight. In just 9 minutes, it identifies critical indicators such as parasite eggs and microorganisms, saving time and labor costs.
  • Urine:Eliminating the need for traditional centrifugation, the process starts just 15 seconds after sample loading. A full report is generated within 9 minutes, enabling timely support for diagnosing urinary system diseases and significantly enhancing clinical responsiveness.
  • Pleural Fluid:With AI-powered recognition and counting of cellular morphology in pleural and ascitic fluids, the system assists veterinarians in determining fluid characteristics, offering multi-dimensional diagnostic insights for complex cases.
 

AI-powered device

By integrating microfluidic automated smearing and liquid-based rapid staining, the system significantly enhances the standardization of sample preparation and reduces operational errors. Equipped with an AI-powered recognition system, it enables rapid classification and provides diagnostic suggestions. Combined with high-definition imaging and standardized report output, it greatly improves diagnostic efficiency.
